Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 10, 2025

1. Introduction

Workstation AI Agent ("we", "our", or "the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension for browser automation and workflow management.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Workflow Configurations: Workflows and automation scripts you create are stored locally in your browser
  • Extension Settings: Your preferences such as backend URL, poll intervals, and feature toggles
  • Execution History: Records of workflows you've executed (stored locally)
  • Template Selections: Which workflow templates you use

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Browser Information: Browser type and version (for compatibility)
  • Extension Usage: Which features you use (tabs, templates, workflows)
  • Error Logs: Technical error information to improve stability (if error reporting enabled)
  • Performance Metrics: Extension load times and resource usage (if monitoring enabled)

2.3 Information NOT Collected

We DO NOT collect:
  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • Browsing history or URLs you visit
  • Passwords or authentication credentials
  • Credit card or payment information
  • Content from web pages you automate
  • Location data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Functionality: To provide workflow automation and execution features
  • Persistence: To save your workflows, settings, and history locally
  • Improvement: To identify and fix bugs, improve performance
  • Support: To help troubleshoot issues you may encounter

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All workflow data, settings, and history are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to sync with a backend server.

4.2 Optional Backend Integration

If you configure a backend server URL in settings:

  • Workflow execution requests are sent to YOUR backend server
  • Authentication is handled via JWT tokens
  • All communication uses HTTPS encryption (recommended)
  • You control the backend server and its data policies

4.3 Security Measures

  • Content Security Policy (CSP): Prevents XSS attacks
  • Input Validation: All user inputs are validated and sanitized
  • Secure Communication: Backend connections use JWT authentication
  • No Third-Party Tracking: No analytics or tracking scripts

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We DO NOT sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

5.1 No Third-Party Services

The extension does not use:

  • Google Analytics or tracking services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Third-party data processors

5.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process, but only to the extent data is available to us.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Data Access

You can access all your data stored by the extension:

  • Workflows: Visible in the History tab
  • Settings: Accessible in the Settings tab
  • Browser Storage: Via Chrome DevTools (Application > Storage)

6.2 Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time:

  • Workflow History: Click "Clear All" in the History tab
  • All Extension Data: Uninstall the extension from Chrome
  • Browser Storage: Clear via Chrome Settings > Privacy > Clear browsing data

6.3 Opt-Out Options

  • Error Reporting: Can be disabled in Settings (if implemented)
  • Auto-Updates: Can be controlled via extension settings
  • Backend Sync: Simply don't configure a backend URL

7. Permissions Explanation

The extension requests the following permissions:

7.1 Required Permissions

  • activeTab: To execute automation workflows on the current browser tab
  • storage: To save your workflows, settings, and execution history locally
  • scripting: To inject automation scripts into web pages you choose to automate
  • notifications: To notify you when workflows complete or encounter errors

7.2 Host Permissions

  • <all_urls>: Necessary to automate workflows on any website you choose. The extension only acts when YOU trigger a workflow.
  • localhost:3000: To communicate with the optional backend server if configured

8. Children's Privacy

The extension is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us.

9. International Users

The extension stores all data locally on your device. If you configure a backend server, data may be transmitted to that server's location. You are responsible for compliance with your local data protection laws.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the extension description
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Showing a notification in the extension (if significant changes)

Your continued use of the extension after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Data Retention

Data is retained locally in your browser until you delete it:

  • Workflow History: Until you clear it or uninstall the extension
  • Settings: Until you change them or uninstall the extension
  • Error Logs: Automatically cleared after 7 days (if error reporting enabled)

This extension is designed to comply with:

  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - EU
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) - USA
  • Other applicable data protection laws
